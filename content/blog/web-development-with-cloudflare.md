I purchased a domain name from Namesilo.

![Namesilo Domain Purchase](/photos/wdwc1.png)

Then log in to Cloudflare and click "Domains."

![Cloudflare Dashboard](/photos/wdwc2.png)

Click "Onboard a Domain," enter the domain you want to host, and follow the steps to get two NS records.

![Onboard a Domain](/photos/wdwc3.png)

Go back to Namesilo with the two NS records you received, delete all the original NS records, enter the two new NS records, and save. This step is part of the hosting process. I've already completed this step. You can now add your own records.

![Namesilo NS Records](/photos/wdwc4.png)

Thank you for watching.
